About

I like products that feel clear, fast, and ready to use.

My work sits between engineering and product thinking, with a strong bias toward clean execution and launch-ready polish.

I'm a Computer Engineering student who enjoys turning ideas into products people can actually use. That includes iOS apps with in-app purchases, web experiences with custom domains, and backend services that support the whole flow.

I care about how a product feels as much as how it works. Clean interfaces, sensible architecture, solid deployment, and thoughtful details are usually where I spend the most energy.

/01

Design with intent

Interfaces should feel calm, readable, and useful before they try to feel impressive.

/02

Build the whole flow

From frontend and iOS to backend integrations, I like understanding how every layer supports the product.

/03

Polish after launch

Shipping is the baseline. Refinement, iteration, and reliability are what make a project feel mature.
